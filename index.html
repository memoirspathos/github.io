metamorphosis (select spreads/24 pages)

metamorphosis is a 24-page zine riso printed in blue, fluorescent pink,
yellow, and flat gold; pages are hydraulic cut and covers are laser cut;
photos taken and edited by hami; writing by hami

25/25 1st ed. copies sold, limited misprints available (DM on instagram for
inquiry or purchase); originally sold on a sliding scale of $22-25 and
$18-25 for qtpoc; all money going to fund hami’s top surgery + post-op
recovery (now their top surgery date is confirmed to be may 15 2023)
